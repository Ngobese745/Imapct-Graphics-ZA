## 5. DATA RETENTION

### 5.1 Retention Periods

**Active Accounts:**
- Account data: Retained while account is active
- Transaction history: 7 years (tax compliance)
- Chat messages: 2 years
- Support tickets: 3 years
- Analytics data: 2 years

**Deleted Accounts:**
- Most data deleted within 30 days
- Transaction records: Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
- Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely

### 5.2 Data Deletion Process

**User-Initiated Deletion:**
1. Navigate to Settings > Account
2. Select "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion
4. Data deleted within 30 days

**What Gets Deleted:**
- Profile information
- Personal details
- Wallet balance forfeited
- Loyalty points removed
- Chat history
- Preferences and settings

**What We Retain:**
- Transaction records (legal requirement)
- Invoices and tax documents
- Fraud prevention data (anonymized)
- Aggregated analytics (anonymized)

### 5.3 Retention Justifications

**Legal Compliance:**
- Tax records: 7 years (SARS requirement)
- Financial transactions: 5-7 years
- Contract records: 3 years after completion

**Business Operations:**
- Fraud prevention: 3 years
- Dispute resolution: 3 years
- Service improvement: 2 years

## 7. YOUR RIGHTS (POPIA COMPLIANCE)

Under POPIA, you have the right to:

### 7.1 Right to Access
- Request copy of your personal information
- Understand how we use your data
- Response within 30 days

### 7.2 Right to Rectification
- Correct inaccurate information
- Update incomplete data
- Can be done through app settings

### 7.3 Right to Deletion
- Request deletion of your data
- Subject to legal retention requirements
- Processed within 30 days

### 7.4 Right to Restriction
- Limit how we process your data
- Object to certain processing activities

### 7.5 Right to Object
- Object to marketing communications
- Object to automated decision-making
- Object to profiling

### 7.6 Right to Data Portability
- Receive your data in portable format
- Transfer data to another provider

### 7.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
- Withdraw marketing consent anytime
- Unsubscribe from emails
- Disable push notifications

### 7.8 Right to Lodge Complaint
- Contact our Information Officer
- File complaint with Information Regulator:
  - **Website:** www.justice.gov.za/inforeg
  - **Email:** inforeg@justice.gov.za
  - **Phone:** 012 406 4818
